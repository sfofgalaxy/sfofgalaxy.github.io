I launched [Awesome Audio Security Research](https://sfofgalaxy.github.io/awesome-audio-security-research/), a website for tracking audio security research and related resources.

The content is maintained through my weekly updated GitHub repository, [sfofgalaxy/awesome-audio-security-research](https://github.com/sfofgalaxy/awesome-audio-security-research). I hope it can make it easier to follow recent work around audio security, including attacks, defenses, benchmarks, and useful reading material.
